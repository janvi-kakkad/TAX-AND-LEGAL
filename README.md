# Agentic AI for Structured Indian Case Law Intelligence  
### Deloitte Hacksplosion 2026 — Tax & Legal Track

---

## Overview

This project presents an AI-powered legal intelligence platform that transforms unstructured Indian legal case documents into structured, searchable, and insight-ready data.

---

## Problem

Legal case judgments in India are lengthy, unstructured, and written in complex legal language. Extracting relevant insights requires extensive manual effort, making legal research time-consuming, inconsistent, and difficult to scale.

---

## Solution

The proposed system automates legal document understanding using a multi-agent AI pipeline. It processes case PDFs and converts them into structured intelligence that can be easily explored through a user-friendly interface.

Key capabilities include:

- Automated extraction of legal entities and case metadata  
- Identification of issues, arguments, and evidence  
- Detection of laws, sections, and precedents  
- Structured representation of court reasoning  
- Chronological case timeline generation  

---

The system uses a parallel multi-agent pipeline where specialized AI agents extract different layers of legal intelligence and aggregate them into a unified structured format.

---

## Key Features

- **Legal Analytics Dashboard**  
  Provides macro-level insights such as case trends, win rates, and most cited laws.

- **Case Intelligence View**  
  Displays structured insights for individual cases across multiple analytical dimensions.

- **AI-Powered Legal Tools**  
  Includes smart legal search, debate simulation, legal brief generation, and case comparison.

- **Multilingual Support**  
  Automatically detects and translates regional language case documents.

- **Duplicate Detection**  
  Prevents reprocessing of already ingested cases.
